# Nobody Puts BloodHound in a Corner: Infusing APM into Your Identity Protection Strategy

**Speaker:** Aaron Woland

## Abstract

Take your BloodHound for a walk! Move beyond silos of identity security tools and data and into a more actionable, context-aware identity protection world, whether you start from an ITDR, SIEM, or XDR perspective.

Learn practical strategies for feeding attack path details into your SIEM for enhanced correlation and alerting, and how to leverage XDR's automation capabilities to shut down attack vectors identified by BloodHound. Discover the true value of connecting these powerhouses, moving beyond basic threat detection to proactively identify and remediate critical identity vulnerabilities before adversaries can exploit them.
