# Not Just Cookies: Modern Session Abuse

**Speaker:** Antero Guy and Andrew Gomez

## Abstract

Modern red teams are frequently tasked with gaining access to sensitive enterprise systems, whether that’s a code repository or a cloud management console. These systems often sit behind Single Sign-On (SSO) and incorporate device-bound authentication. These cookies are often highly short-lived making the classic cookie theft and replay approach no longer sufficient.

This talk walks through how attackers and red teams approach browser-based persistence and compromise user sessions to gain access to target applications. We’ll explore Chromium app-bound encryption, and scenarios where SSO solutions such as Azure Primary Refresh Tokens (PRTs), Seamless SSO, and Federated services  can be leveraged to obtain access to high value targets.

Through live demonstrations and controlled case studies, we’ll show how APTs realistically pivot from local browsers to cloud-hosted applications, and more importantly, how defenders can detect and prevent these techniques
