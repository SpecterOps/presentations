# Keynote | Vision of Attack Path Management and the Road Ahead

**Speaker:** Jared Atkinson

## Abstract

Attack Path Management as a practice has seen a lot of growth over the past year and has been shaped by research, product innovation, and real-world offensive engagements. In this session, SpecterOps leadership shares highlights from the past year—including OpenGraph-driven community innovation, internal research breakthroughs, and war stories from engagements that pushed attack path thinking in new directions. We’ll close with a forward-looking vision for how Attack Path Management will mature over the next year, driven by the continuous feedback loop between product, research, and services.
